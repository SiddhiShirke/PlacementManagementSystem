    Placement Management System

    Placement Management System is a web application. For the placement and training department in the college. This application helps to manage the student information with regards to the placements. This system helps the students, company to register and communicate all the information in the portal. The Placement Coordinator will collect the information from various companies who want to recruit the students and updates to the students from time to time. And also arranges the profile of students according to various streams. The Placement Coordinator will clearly notify the needs and requirements of the company.
